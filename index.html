MOUNTAINS

Kasauli

A quintessential hill station surrounded by pine and oak forests with scenic viewpoints and colonial-era landmarks, Kasauli stands at an altitude of 1,800 metres.

Mussoorie

Mussoorie is a scenic hill station dotted with mesmerizing waterfalls, towering mountain peaks, and a string of colonial buildings.

Dalhousie

Dalhousie is a popular hill station spread out over five hills in Himachal Pradesh boasting views of the snow-capped peaks of the Dhauladhar ranges.

BEACHES

Palolem Beach

Palolem Beach is a crescent-shaped beach in South Goa, India, known for its white sand, calm waters, and palm trees.

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam is located 16 km from Thiruvananthapuram city, the capital of Kerala. This shallow beach with three adjacent crescent beaches offer leisure options.

Marina Beach

A splendidly serene strip of coast along the Bay of Bengal, the Marina Beach is considered to be one of the finest beaches in India.

MONUMENTS

Taj Mahal

A white marble mausoleum in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Hawa Mahal

A five-story pink sandstone palace in Jaipur, Rajasthan, built in the 18th century by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh. It is also known as the "Palace of the Winds".

Red Fort

A massive red sandstone fort in Delhi, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

WATERFALLS

Nohkalikai Falls

The tallest waterfall in India, with a height of 340 meters. It's located near Cherrapunji, which is known for being one of the wettest places in the world.

Dudhsagar Falls

A tiered waterfall in South Goa District, with a height of 310 meters. It's known for its milky white appearance, especially during monsoon season.

Khandadhar Falls

The second highest waterfall in Odisha, with a height of 244 meters. It's located in the Keonjhar district.
